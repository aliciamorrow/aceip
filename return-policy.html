Return Policy

Our Generous Returns Process

We’ll refund you for the item’s cost for up to 365 days after you place your order if it’s returned to us in the same state as when we shipped it out.

Simply email us to initiate a return. The return address is listed on the shipment you received.

We recommend shipping to us with a trackable shipping method so you can stay on top of the timeline of your return. If you’re in the USA, we recommend USPS First Class shipping, which is inexpensive and provides a tracking number. Pack items securely in a sturdy box and cushion with packing bubbles or newspaper.
